> [!NOTE]
> To clarify, these only work with games which don't filter out **Unity Rich Text Tags**.
> There are also many ways to do the same thing, so I have listed all of them here.
---

### Italics
```<i>text</i>```
```<i>text```

### Bold
```<b>text</b>```
```<b>```

### Underline
```<u>text</u>```
```<u>```

### Strikethrough (Newer Unity only)
```<s>text</s>```
```<s>```

### Coloring
```<color=#RRGGBB>text</color>```  
```<#RRGGBB>text```
```<#RGB>text```  

### Transparency (00-FF)
```<alpha=#AA>text</alpha>```
```<alpha=#AA>text```  

### Size
```<size=10>text</size>```  
```<size=150%>text```

### Highlighting and Background Colour
```<mark=#RRGGBB>text</mark>```
```<mark=#RGB>text</mark>```
```<mark=#RRGGBB>text```
```<mark=#RGB>text```

### Spacing
```<cspace=1>text</cspace>```
```<cspace=1>text```

#### Monospacing
```<mspace=1>text</mspace>```
```<mspace=1>text```

### Offset
```<voffset=10>text</voffset>```
```<voffset=10>text```

### Alignment
```<align=left>text</align>```
```<align=center>text</align>```
```<align=right>text</align>```
```<align=left>text```
```<align=center>text```
```<align=right>text```

### Casing
```<uppercase>text</uppercase>```
```<lowercase>text</lowercase>```
```<smallcaps>text</smallcaps>```
```<uppercase>text```
```<lowercase>text```
```<smallcaps>text```

### Font (Might not work in alot of games)
```<font="FontName">text</font>```
```<font="FontName">text```
